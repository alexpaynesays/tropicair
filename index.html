Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How do I know if my HVAC system needs servicing?

Signs that your system may need servicing include unusual noises, inconsistent temperatures, or increased energy bills.

Q2: What should I look for when hiring an HVAC contractor?

Check for licensing, insurance, customer reviews, and experience with your specific type of system.

Q3: How often should I have my HVAC system inspected?

It's recommended to have an inspection twice a year-once before summer and once before winter.

Q4: Can I perform any maintenance on my own?

Yes! Regularly changing air filters and keeping outdoor units clear of debris can help maintain efficiency.
